# Public source notes for the Klarna launch demo

The application does not scrape or reproduce customer posts. Synthetic feedback was written from recurring themes found in public product documentation and community discussion.

## Official product and launch facts

- eBay announced the U.S. expansion of its Klarna partnership on April 23, 2025.
- The U.S. experience includes Pay in 4 and longer-term financing for eligible orders.
- Eligibility varies by factors such as order value, address, currency, shipping origin, category, delivery method, app version, and Klarna approval.
- Returns are initiated through eBay, while Klarna adjusts or refunds the payment plan.

## Recurring public discussion themes used for synthetic examples

- Klarna appearing on a listing but not at checkout
- buyer and seller confusion about when an item ships and how sellers are paid
- uncertainty about declines and eligibility
- questions about returns, refunds, and remaining installments
- interest and total-cost visibility for longer-term financing
- positive reactions to payment flexibility and simple payment management

The synthetic records should be replaced with representative, consented research and operational data before making real business decisions.
